Canada Work Guide

Expansive 3-Year Working Holiday (IEC) for British Citizens

New Opportunities in the Great White North

A comprehensive guide for United Kingdom citizens wishing to live and work in Canada.

New IEC Rules - Age 18-35 & 3-Year Duration

Under the latest agreement, UK citizens aged 18-35 (inclusive) can now apply for the Working Holiday category for an unprecedented stay of up to 36 months (3 years). This Open Work Permit allows you to work for any employer in any location. The quota has also been increased, making Canada a premier destination for young British professionals and adventurers.

Visa Categories

Working Holiday

The premier choice. Open work permit for up to 3 years. No job offer needed to join the candidate pool.

Young Professionals

For skilled UK citizens with a job offer that contributes to their professional development.

Express Entry

The federal system for permanent residency. Highly skilled UK workers with 3 years of IEC experience are strong candidates.

General Requirements (IEC)

Valid British Passport
Proof of funds (CAD $2,500)
Comprehensive Health Insurance for the FULL 36 months
Biometrics (collected at VFS Global centers in the UK)
Police Certificate (ACRO check)

Application Steps

1
Create an IRCC Profile

Submit your profile to the IEC pool. Ensure you select the for-UK pool and Working Holiday category.

2
Wait for invitation (ITA)

Draws happen frequently. If you receive an ITA, accept it within 10 days.

3
Apply for Work Permit

Complete the online forms, upload your ACRO and scans, and pay fees (approx. CAD $350).

4
Port of Entry Letter

Upon approval, you get a POE letter. Bring this to the border in Canada to receive your 3-year permit.
