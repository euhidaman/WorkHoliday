Work & Residency Pathways for Canada Citizens

Relocating to Sweden

Official information based on current research for Canada citizens wishing to live and work in Sweden.

Available Visa Types

Sweden allows young adults to combine holidays with temporary work. As a citizen of Canada, you may be eligible to apply for the Working Holiday Visa.

Official Requirements

Valid Canada Passport
Age Limit: 18-30
Financial Requirement: SEK 15,000
Comprehensive Travel & Health Insurance
Return flight ticket or sufficient funds to purchase one

Application Process

1. Check Eligibility & Quotas

Ensure the quota for Canada citizens is currently open for the Sweden program.

2. Gather Documents

Prepare your passport, proof of funds, background check, and medical exams if required.

3. Apply via Official Portal

Submit your application online or at the Sweden embassy/consulate in your jurisdiction.

4. Visa Grant & Arrival

Once approved, activate your visa by entering Sweden within the required timeframe.

Official Immigration Source

Portal: migrationsverket.se

Please consult the official Sweden immigration website or your nearest embassy for the most up-to-date and legally binding information before applying.
