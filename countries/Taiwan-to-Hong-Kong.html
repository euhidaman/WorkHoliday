Hong Kong Work Guide

Work & Residency Pathways for Taiwan Residents

Relocating to Hong Kong

Information based on current research for Taiwan residents wishing to live and work in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region.

Available Visa Types for Taiwan Residents

Taiwan residents can apply for the Working Holiday Scheme, the Top Talent Pass Scheme (TTPS), or the Admission Scheme for Mainland Talents and Professionals (ASMTP). Hong Kong's proximity and similar business culture make it a top choice for Taiwanese professionals.

Main Work Routes

TTPS

Top Talent Pass Scheme. For earners with HK$2.5M+ income or graduates from top 100 global unis (including several in Taiwan). Enter first, work later.

ASMTP (Employment)

Admission Scheme for Mainland Talents and Professionals. Despite the name, this scheme is the primary route for Taiwan residents with professional offers.

Working Holiday

Allows Taiwan youth (18-30) to work and travel for 1 year. Annual quota: 800 places. One of the most popular short-term options.

General Requirements

Valid Taiwan Passport (with ID number)
Bachelor's degree or professional certificate
Confirmed Employment Offer (Market-rate salary)
No criminal record or Adverse reputation
Savings proof (if applying for WHV or TTPS)

Typical Process

1
Online Application

Submit your application via the HK Immigration Department (ImmD) e-Services. GEP/ASMTP requires employer documents.

2
ImmD Assessment

Assessment takes roughly 4 weeks for TTPS and 6-8 weeks for ASMTP/GEP.

3
E-Visa Issuance

Download the "Electronic Visa" once approved. Note that you may need a "Taiwan Resident Entry Permit" to enter depending on your status.

4
HKID Application

Apply for a Hong Kong Identity Card (HKID) at the Immigration Tower in Wan Chai within 30 days of arrival.

Hong Kong Immigration Department (ImmD)

Immigration Tower, 7 Gloucester Road, Wan Chai, HK

Central authority for entry permits and residence status

immd.gov.hk

Service Note

Taiwan residents with professional roles should ensure their employers are registered in HK. The TTPS is particularly advantageous for graduates of NTU, NTHU, etc.
