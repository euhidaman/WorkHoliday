United Kingdom Work Guide

Professional & Skilled Pathways for Austrian Citizens

Relocating to Great Britain

A comprehensive guide for Austrian citizens wishing to live and work in the UK post-Brexit.

Skilled Worker Visa System

Since Brexit, there is no automatic right to work for Austrians in the UK. There is no Youth Mobility / Working Holiday scheme for Austria. The primary route is the Skilled Worker Visa, which requires a job offer from a licensed UK employer. Alternatively, the High Potential Individual (HPI) visa is available for graduates of top global universities. Standard visits for tourism/meetings are visa-free for up to 6 months.

Visa Categories

Skilled Worker

Requires a job offer from a licensed sponsor, a Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS), and meeting salary requirements.

HPI Visa

For those who graduated from a top-50 global university in the last 5 years. No job offer needed to apply.

Health & Care

A fast-track, low-fee visa for medical professionals with a job offer in the NHS or social care sector.

General Requirements

Valid Austrian Passport
Certificate of Sponsorship (from a UK employer)
English Language Proficiency (B1 level or degree taught in English)
Proof of Savings (£1,270)
Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) payment

Application Steps

1
Secure a Job Offer

Apply for roles in the UK. The employer must be a licensed sponsor and willing to issue a CoS.

2
Apply Online

Submit your visa application on GOV.UK. You'll need your CoS reference number and proof of English.

3
Pay IHS & Fees

Pay the IHS (£1,035/year for most workers) and the visa fee based on your job type and stay length.

4
Identity Check

Use the 'UK Immigration: ID Check' app to scan your passport, or attend a visa center if the app doesn't support you.
