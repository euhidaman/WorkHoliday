Work & Residency Pathways for Malta Citizens

Relocating to Australia

Official Working Holiday Visa information for Malta citizens wishing to live and work in Australia.

Application Portal

Apply via ImmiAccount. As a citizen of Malta, you may be eligible for the Working Holiday Visa to Australia.

Official Requirements

Valid passport (min. 12 months validity)
Age 18–30 (35 for some nationalities)
AUD 5,000 in accessible funds
Comprehensive health insurance for the full stay
Military discharge certificate (if applicable)

How to Apply

1. Create an ImmiAccount at the official Australian immigration portal.
2. Select 'New Application' and then 'Work & Holiday'.
3. Choose the correct subclass: Subclass 417 (Working Holiday) or Subclass 462 (Work and Holiday) based on your country's agreement.
4. Upload your passport, bank statements showing at least AUD 5,000, and military discharge certificate if applicable.
5. Pay the visa application charge and await the decision (usually within a few weeks).
Common Pitfalls & Expert Advice

Choosing the wrong visa subclass (417 vs 462) — always verify which applies to your country's bilateral agreement. Failing to provide translated bank statements if they are not in English. Not disclosing minor criminal convictions or health conditions, which can lead to cancellation.

Australian Embassy / High Commission
