How to read this matrix

  1. Find your passport row — use the filter box above to jump straight to it.
  2. Read across — every ✓ column is a destination where you can apply for a WHV.
  3. Hover any cell to confirm the pair name before clicking through to the full guide.
  4. Each bilateral agreement has its own age limit, annual quota, and required funds — check the destination page for exact requirements.
ⓘ Data reflects published bilateral WHV agreements as of 2026. Requirements (age limits 18–30 or 18–35, annual caps, proof-of-funds, once-per-lifetime rules) vary by pair. Always verify with the official embassy or immigration authority before applying.
