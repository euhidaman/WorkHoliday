Taiwan Work Guide

Working Holiday & Career Migration for British Citizens

Relocating to Taiwan

A comprehensive guide for British citizens wishing to live and work in the beautiful island of Taiwan.

Working Holiday Scheme - 1,000 Places

British citizens aged 18-30 are eligible for the Taiwan Working Holiday Scheme. There is an annual quota of 1,000 places. Successful applicants can stay for up to 12 months (initially 180 days, extendable by another 180 days) and engage in temporary employment or study. For high-level professional migration, the Taiwan Employment Gold Card is the premier 4-in-1 visa route for skilled workers.

Visa Categories

Working Holiday

For UK citizens 18-30. 1,000 places per year. Allows 12 months of work and travel. Extendable once after arrival.

Gold Card

A premium 4-in-1 visa for high-level talents. Allows entry without a job offer and provides high professional flexibility.

Work Permit

Standard employment visa. Requires a job offer from a Taiwan company that sponsors your residence permit (ARC).

General Requirements

Valid British Passport
Proof of Funds (~£2,100 GBP for WHV)
Health Insurance (must cover the duration of the stay)
Character (No prior criminal record)
Return Ticket or funds to buy one

Preparation Steps

1
Online App Form

Complete the Visa Application Form online at the Bureau of Consular Affairs (BOCA) website.

2
Submit In-Person

Bring your form, passport, bank statements, and photos to the Taipei Representative Office in London or Edinburgh.

3
Wait for Approval

Processing typically takes 3-10 working days. You will receive a visa sticker in your passport.

4
Extend in Taiwan

After arrival, before your first 180 days expire, apply for a 180-day extension at the National Immigration Agency in Taiwan.
