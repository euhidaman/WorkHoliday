Youth Mobility Scheme & Professional Pathways for Canadians

Relocating to Great Britain

A comprehensive guide for Canadian citizens wishing to live and work in the United Kingdom.

Available Visa Types for Canadian Citizens

Canadian citizens can apply for the Youth Mobility Scheme, Skilled Worker Visa, or Global Talent Visa. Canadians aged 18-35 are eligible for the Youth Mobility Scheme with a stay of up to 3 years.

Visa Categories

Youth Mobility Scheme

For Canadian citizens aged 18-35. Allows you to live and work in the UK for up to 3 years. One of the most flexible routes for young professionals.

Skilled Worker

Requires a job offer and sponsorship t? a UK employer. Points-based system favoring skilled roles.

HPI Visa

High Potential Individual. For graduates from prestigious global universities outside the UK in the last 5 years.

General Requirements (YMS)

Valid Canadian Passport
Proof of Savings (£2,535)
No children under 18 living with you or for whom you're responsible
Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) payment
TB test (only if residing in high-risk areas recently)

Preparation Steps

1
Online Application

Apply online through GOV.UK. You can apply up to 6 months before you plan to arrive in the UK.

2
Fees & IHS

Pay the visa application fee (£298) and the health surcharge (£776 per year) to use the NHS.

3
ID Check or Appointment

Complete your identity check using the GOV.UK app or at a VFS Global center (Toronto, Vancouver, etc.).

4
Visa Vignette

Wait for approval (usually 3 weeks). You'll receive a visa sticker in your passport to travel to the UK.
