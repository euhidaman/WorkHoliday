Australia Work Guide

Free Movement for New Zealand Citizens

Trans-Tasman Work Rights

Information based on current research regarding rights for New Zealand citizens in Australia.

Visa Not Required for NZ Citizens

Under the Trans-Tasman Travel Agreement, New Zealand citizens generally do not require a visa to work and live in Australia. Work rights are granted automatically upon arrival for eligible New Zealanders.

Key Benefits

New Zealand citizens may work and live in Australia freely. There is no requirement to apply for a standard work visa (like the TSS or Working Holiday) prior to travel.

Unlimited Work

No employer sponsorship required. Work for any employer.

Right to Reside

Live and settle anywhere in Australia indefinitely.

Automatic Grant

Special Category Visa (444) granted electronically on arrival.

Naturalization Pathway

Simplified route to Australian citizenship for NZ residents.

Department of Home Affairs, Australia

Trans-Tasman Travel Arrangement

Subclass 444 (Special Category Visa)

homeaffairs.gov.au

Note for Travelers

Eligible NZ citizens are typically granted a Special Category Visa (subclass 444) on arrival at an Australian airport, provided they meet character and health requirements. You do not need to do anything before you travel except hold a valid NZ passport.
