USA Work Guide

Work & Residency Pathways for British Citizens

Relocating to the USA

Information based on current research for British citizens wishing to live and work in the United States.

Available Visa Types for British Citizens

British citizens primarily utilize the H-1B Specialty Occupation Visa, the L-1 Intra-company Transferee Visa, and the O-1 Extraordinary Ability Visa. While ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization) covers short-term business visits, all formal employment requires a specific visa status.

Main Work Routes

H-1B Visa

For professionals with a bachelor's degree. Subject to an annual lottery. Requires a US employer sponsor. Valid for up to 6 years.

L-1 (Transfer)

For employees of UK companies being transferred to a US branch or subsidiary. Streamlined for managers (L-1A) and specialized staff (L-1B).

O-1 Visa

For individuals with extraordinary ability in their field. Common for British talent in creative industries, tech, and healthcare.

General Requirements

Valid British Passport (at least 6 months validity)
Approved Petition Notice (I-797) from USCIS
Degree certificate and professional evidence
Completed DS-160 online form and appointment booking
MRV application fee payment (via debit/credit card)

Typical Process

1
Employer Submission

Your US employer sponsor files the I-129 petition with USCIS in the United States. Wait for the I-797 approval notice.

2
DS-160 & Booking

Fill out the DS-160. Create a profile on the official US visa services site for the UK to pay the fee and book your interview.

3
London Embassy Interview

Attend an interview at the US Embassy in London (Nine Elms). Bring your passport, I-797, and job support letter.

4
Courier Delivery

Once approved, your passport will be returned via the DX courier service within a few working days. You are then free to travel.

U.S. Embassy in London

33 Nine Elms Ln, London SW11 7US

Primary processing hub for all US non-immigrant visas in the United Kingdom

uk.usembassy.gov

Service Note

The Nine Elms embassy is a high-security facility. Large bags and electronics (except mobile phones) are restricted. Ensure you allow extra time for security screening upon arrival.
