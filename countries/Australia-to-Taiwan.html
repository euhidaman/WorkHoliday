Taiwan Work Guide

Work & Residency Pathways for Australian Citizens

Relocating to Taiwan

Information based on current research for Australian citizens wishing to live and work in Taiwan (R.O.C.).

Available Visa Types for Australian Citizens

Australians have access to the Working Holiday Visa, the Employment Gold Card, and standard Work Visas. Australians enjoy visa-free entry for tourism but MUST obtain a specific work visa or ARC for employment.

Main Work Routes

Employment Gold Card

A 4-in-1 permit (work, residence, re-entry, and visa). For highly skilled professionals in specialized fields with a salary threshold of approx NT$160,000/month.

Standard Work Visa

Requires a job offer from a Taiwanese company. The employer must first obtain a "Work Permit" from the Ministry of Labor before you apply for the Resident Visa.

Working Holiday

Allows Australians (18-30) to work and travel for 1 year. This is a great way for young people to gain short-term experience in Taiwan.

General Requirements

Valid Australian Passport
Recognized University Degree (Bachelor's or higher)
Ministry of Labor Work Permit (issued to employer)
Health Certificate (signed by a recognized hospital)
Alien Resident Certificate (ARC) application within 15 days of entry

Typical Process

1
Work Permit Approval

Your Taiwan-based employer applies for a work permit from the Ministry of Labor (MOL).

2
Resident Visa Application

Apply for a Resident Visa at a TECO (Taipei Economic and Cultural Office) in Australia (e.g., Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Canberra).

3
Entry & Registration

Travel to Taiwan. You must register for your Alien Resident Certificate (ARC) at the National Immigration Agency (NIA).

4
ARC Issuance

Collect your physical ARC card. This serves as your primary legal IDs for work, bank accounts, and health insurance in Taiwan.

Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) - Australia

Units 8 & 11, 40 Marcus Clarke St, Canberra ACT 2601

Consular offices are also located in Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane

roc-taiwan.org/au_en/

Service Note

For Employment Gold Card applications, everything is handled online via the Foreign Talents Portal. No trip to the consulate is needed for the initial digital certificate.
