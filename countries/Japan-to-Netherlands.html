Netherlands Work Guide

Work & Residency Pathways for Japan Citizens

Relocating to Netherlands

Official information based on current research for Japan citizens wishing to live and work in Netherlands.

Available Visa Types

The Dutch WHP enables young people from partner countries to experience Dutch society. As a citizen of Japan, you may be eligible to apply for the Working Holiday Programme (WHP).

Official Requirements

Valid Japan Passport
Age Limit: 18-30
Financial Requirement: EUR 2,000
Comprehensive Travel & Health Insurance
Return flight ticket or sufficient funds to purchase one

Application Process

1. Check Eligibility & Quotas

Ensure the quota for Japan citizens is currently open for the Netherlands program.

2. Gather Documents

Prepare your passport, proof of funds, background check, and medical exams if required.

3. Apply via Official Portal

Submit your application online or at the Netherlands embassy/consulate in your jurisdiction.

4. Visa Grant & Arrival

Once approved, activate your visa by entering Netherlands within the required timeframe.
