United Kingdom Work Guide

Professional & Skilled Migration for China Residents

Relocating to Great Britain

A comprehensive guide for residents of Mainland China wishing to live and work in the United Kingdom.

Skilled Worker Visa System

There is currently no Youth Mobility (Working Holiday) agreement for residents of Mainland China. The primary path for employment is the Skilled Worker Visa, which requires a job offer from a licensed UK sponsor. Highly accomplished individuals may apply for the Global Talent visa, and graduates of top global universities (e.g., Tsinghua, Peking Uni) may qualify for the HPI Visa. All stays over 6 months require a TB test.

Visa Categories

Skilled Worker

Requires a job offer and sponsorship from a UK employer. Points-based system favoring roles in shortage or high salary.

HPI Visa

High Potential Individual. For graduates from selected global top-50 universities outside the UK in the past 5 years.

Global Talent

For world-class talent in digital tech, research, or arts. Does not require a job offer to apply.
